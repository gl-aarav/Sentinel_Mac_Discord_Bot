Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-08-19

This policy explains how the Sentinel Discord application (“App”) handles information.

What We Collect

  • Basic interaction data (e.g., command names, timestamps) for reliability and abuse prevention.
  • Error logs and performance metrics.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect message content unless explicitly required by a command and with user intent.
  • We do not sell or share data with third parties for advertising.

Retention

Logs are retained for a limited period and then deleted or aggregated.

Your Choices

Server owners can request deletion of stored logs by contacting us.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes means you accept the revised policy.
